Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty and delicious Italian pasta dish made with a rich, slow-cooked meat sauce. This recipe is perfect for family dinners and can be prepared with simple ingredients you likely already have at home.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: 1 hour

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until soft and fragrant.
  3. Stir in ground beef, cook until browned.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil. Stir well.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain.
  7. Serve spaghetti topped with sauce and sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Tips & Notes

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer for longer. You can also substitute ground beef with ground turkey for a lighter version. Leftover sauce tastes even better the next day!
